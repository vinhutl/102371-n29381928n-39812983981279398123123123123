What Is Night?

Night started as a website called "secret.gg" which I made in 6th grade, featuring over 200 opensocial games. After about 2 1/2 years, I decided to expand it to a real website to be used by anyone. This website improves every day, so I hope you have fun!

Inspiration

This site was inspired by 3kh0 (RIP), Selenite, Seraph, Interstellar, and Doge Unblocker. Thank you so much for the inspiration!
